Across the skies of Talmera soars a lofty sun, bringing life to the meadows and villages below.  It is, as it has always been, a place of peace.

But darkness creeps toward the eastern shores.

The Black Riders, robed in shadows, approach from beyond the sea, bringing chaos and devastation to unsuspecting Talmerians.

Our hero finds himself poised on the cusp of fate, destined to face a measure of evil the likes of which he has never known.  The tides of Talmera are turning.  A perilous world awaits.
